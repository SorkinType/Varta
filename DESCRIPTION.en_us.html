Varta is a Sans typeface for Devnagari and Latin scripts. Varta offers a compelling sensibility made possible by the clever fusing of warm humanity with distinct clarity and professional crispness. As Varta is seen at larger sizes its congenial personality emerges.

Varta is well suited to a broad range of tasks including sign systems, editorial design, packaging and extended reading on screens. Varta is offered in four of the four most commonly used weights to ensure broad utility. Each weight has been designed to render well down to surprisingly small sizes and on both low and high-quality screens.
